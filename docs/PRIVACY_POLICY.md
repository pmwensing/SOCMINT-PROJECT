# Privacy Policy

SOCMINT Workbench is designed for evidence-first, case-scoped investigations.

## Data collected by the application

- User account information required for login and administration.
- Case records, subjects, seeds, observations, assertions, artifacts, audit events, and exports entered or generated by authorized operators.
- Usage and quota events needed for membership and billing administration.

## Data handling principles

- Minimize collection to the documented case scope.
- Preserve source references and custody metadata for evidence artifacts.
- Use redaction for sensitive exports.
- Keep secrets, databases, logs, backups, and exports out of public repositories.

## Operator responsibilities

Operators are responsible for legal authority, source authorization, secure deployment, retention decisions, and disclosure rules for their jurisdiction and use case.

## Security expectations

Use strong secrets, secure cookies, encrypted backups, restricted admin access, and the built-in hardening and release-readiness endpoints before beta use.
