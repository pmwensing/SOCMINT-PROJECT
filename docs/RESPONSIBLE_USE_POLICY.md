# Responsible Use Policy

SOCMINT Workbench is for authorized investigations using lawful, public, open-source, or user-provided information.

## Allowed

- Case-scoped research with documented authorization.
- Public/open-source connector runs.
- Evidence capture with source references and custody notes.
- Analyst review before assertions are promoted or exported.
- Export only after preflight review.

## Not allowed

- Credential theft or credential stuffing.
- Bypassing access controls.
- Circumventing platform protections.
- Unauthorized login-gated collection.
- Private-data access without authorization.
- Harassment, stalking, doxxing, or intimidation.
- Paid-tier use to bypass legal, ethical, or responsible-use gates.

## Operator duties

- Record case purpose and authorization.
- Minimize collection to the investigation scope.
- Review observations before assertion promotion.
- Use redaction and export preflight before sharing.
- Stop and escalate when scope is unclear.
